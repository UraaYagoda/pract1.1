Services

Website Development

Web development, also known as website development, refers to the tasks associated with creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications that run online on a browser. It may, however, also include web design, web programming, and database management.

Website Design

It is a field that includes the creation and design of web pages, including graphics, colors, fonts, and interface components. Web design is important to ensure that websites are user-friendly and attractive

Website Deployment

Deployment in software and web development means pushing changes or updates from one deployment environment to another. When setting up a website you will always have your live website, which is called the live environment or production environment.

SEO

The process of adjusting the HTML code, text content (content), site structure, control of external factors to meet the requirements of the algorithm of search engines , with the aim of raising the position of the site in the search results of these systems according to certain user requests.

DevOps

Methodology for automating the technological processes of assembling, configuring and deploying software. The methodology involves active interaction between development specialists and information technology services specialists and the mutual integration of their technological processes into each other to ensure high quality software products .

QA

Are actions performed on a product during the development process to obtain information about its current state: how ready the product is and whether it meets quality requirements at any given time.
